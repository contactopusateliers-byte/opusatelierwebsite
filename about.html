Our Story

Opus Atelier Ltd is a UK-registered company (Company No: 16838652) founded in 2023, specializing in affordable, custom fashion clothing. We use only synthetic, cotton, and blend materials to create unique garments for global customers.

Our mission: Provide stylish, low-cost clothing without the use of luxury fabrics or high-value designs, ensuring accessibility and compliance with international financial standards. We ship to over 50 countries, with a focus on cross-border e-commerce.

Team: A small group of designers in London, using eco-friendly non-luxury materials. No high-value items to avoid regulatory issues.
