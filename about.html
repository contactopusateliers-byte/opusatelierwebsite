Our Story

Opus Atelier Ltd is a UK-registered company (Company No: 16838652) founded in 2025, specializing in affordable, custom fashion jewelry. We use only synthetic and non-precious materials to create unique accessories for global customers.

Our mission: Provide stylish, low-cost jewelry without the use of precious metals or stones, ensuring accessibility and compliance with international financial standards. We ship to over 50 countries, with a focus on cross-border e-commerce.

Team: A small group of designers in London, using eco-friendly synthetic materials. No high-value items to avoid regulatory issues.
